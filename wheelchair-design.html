Project Details

This is my project from the Engineering Graphics in Design course at UC Davis. I collaborated with three other engineering students to model a wheelchair in SolidWorks based on dimensions taken from a physical model. The final CAD model included over 20 parts. I was mainly in charge of assembling the main frame of the wheelchair.
