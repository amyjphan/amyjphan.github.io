Project Details

This is my project from the Engineering Design course at UC Davis. I worked in a team of four to design and create the prototype for a device to potentially be used at the UC Davis Student Farm. I created the CAD model and had a big role in constructing the physical model. Our final prototype included a rotating plate mechanism, weight sensor and LED indicator, which was all solar-powered and controlled by an Arduino Uno.
